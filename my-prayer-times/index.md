My Prayer Times is an iPhone and iPad app for Muslims who want a clear daily
view of prayer times, tahrim guidance, and Qibla direction from their current
location.

There are already many apps that can remind us of our daily prayers and point
us toward the Qibla. I created My Prayer Times because I was interested in the
calculations and falak methods used to determine these times, and because I
wanted the app to show the assumptions behind its results in a simple way.

As a Muslim in Malaysia, I also have to pay attention to tahrim times. Many
apps do not show these intervals clearly, and some users may not be fully
aware of them. My Prayer Times presents prayer times and tahrim guidance
together, while reminding users that this is timing guidance, not a fatwa.

The app is provided free as my amal jariah for Muslim users. Suggestions,
corrections, and thoughtful feedback are most welcome so the app can continue
to improve, inshaAllah.

- No account, advertising, analytics, or tracking
- A Today dashboard with local date, place, next prayer, countdown, and full schedule
- Coordinate-based estimates for Imsak, Subuh, Syuruk, Dhuha, Zohor, Asar, Maghrib, and Isyak
- Tahrim intervals where the selected method supports the app's Sunni tahrim model
- A dedicated Qibla tab with bearing, heading, compass accuracy, and north reference
- Automatic or manual selection from 13 calculation authorities and conventions
- English, Bahasa Melayu, or System Default language selection
- Optional local notifications for Subuh, Zohor, Asar, Maghrib, and Isyak
- Small, medium, and large Home Screen widgets plus Lock Screen accessories
- Designed for iOS 17 and later

## A Clear Daily View

The **Today** tab keeps the information needed through the day close at hand:
your detected place, local date, the next prayer, a live countdown, current
tahrim status, and the complete schedule. The display changes gently with the
part of the day while preserving readable system styling.

Tahrim guidance appears beside the prayer schedule so it is not treated as an
afterthought. The app currently shows intervals around Subuh, sunrise, solar
noon, Asar, and sunset for methods that use the app's Sunni tahrim model. It
also explains when a selected Jafari preset does not define those intervals.

The **Qibla** tab gives the compass its own focused space. It calculates the
Qibla bearing geodesically and combines it with the device compass, showing
your heading, accuracy, and whether true or magnetic north is being used.

## Calculation Methods

My Prayer Times includes presets for JAKIM, Muslim World League, Umm al-Qura,
Egyptian Survey, Karachi, ISNA, Kemenag Indonesia, Diyanet, MUIS, Grande
Mosquee de Paris, UOIF / Musulmans de France, University of Tehran, and Leva
Qum.

Automatic by Location selects a matching national convention for supported
countries and uses Umm al-Qura as the fallback. You can also choose a method
manually, and that choice remains active across launches and location changes
until you return to automatic selection.

Each displayed time is a coordinate-based estimate using the selected
convention. Official zone, locality, mosque, or religious-authority timetables
should be followed when a difference matters. Some presets are labelled as
compatibility estimates because public schedules may include institutional,
seasonal, locality, or rounding rules that a simple coordinate calculation
cannot fully reproduce.

My Prayer Times is not affiliated with or endorsed by any authority or
organisation named in the app.

## Notifications and Widgets

Prayer notifications are optional. If you enable them, the app creates local
notifications on the device for the prayers you choose from Subuh, Zohor,
Asar, Maghrib, and Isyak. iOS manages delivery, and reopening the app
periodically renews the rolling schedule.

The widgets use the latest snapshot saved by the main app: location, time
zone, selected calculation method, language, and save time. Small and medium
widgets focus on the next prayer and upcoming times, while the large widget
shows the full schedule with tahrim context. Lock Screen accessories provide a
compact next-prayer view.

## Privacy

The app does not require an account and does not include advertising,
analytics, or cross-app tracking. Location is used while the app is in use to
calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. The app does not request
background location access and does not upload prayer schedules, compass
readings, or notification data to the developer.

## Paparan Harian Yang Jelas

Tab **Hari Ini** mengutamakan maklumat yang diperlukan sepanjang hari: tempat
yang dikesan, tarikh setempat, solat seterusnya, kira detik langsung, status
tahrim semasa dan jadual lengkap. Paparan berubah secara lembut mengikut fasa
hari sambil mengekalkan gaya sistem yang mudah dibaca.

Panduan tahrim dipaparkan bersama jadual solat supaya ia tidak terpisah
daripada kegunaan harian. Aplikasi kini menunjukkan selang sekitar Subuh,
matahari terbit, tengah hari suria, Asar dan matahari terbenam bagi kaedah
yang menggunakan model tahrim Sunni aplikasi. Aplikasi juga menerangkan apabila
pratetap Jaafari yang dipilih tidak mentakrifkan selang tersebut.

Tab **Kiblat** memberikan ruang khusus untuk kompas. Aplikasi mengira bearing
kiblat secara geodesik dan menggabungkannya dengan kompas peranti, termasuk
arah semasa, ketepatan dan sama ada utara benar atau utara magnet digunakan.

## Kaedah Pengiraan

My Prayer Times menyediakan pratetap JAKIM, Muslim World League, Umm al-Qura,
Egyptian Survey, Karachi, ISNA, Kemenag Indonesia, Diyanet, MUIS, Grande
Mosquee de Paris, UOIF / Musulmans de France, University of Tehran dan Leva
Qum.

Automatik mengikut Lokasi memilih konvensyen kebangsaan yang sepadan untuk
negara yang disokong dan menggunakan Umm al-Qura sebagai pilihan sandaran.
Anda juga boleh memilih kaedah secara manual, dan pilihan itu kekal digunakan
selepas aplikasi dibuka semula atau lokasi berubah sehingga anda kembali
kepada pemilihan automatik.

Setiap waktu yang dipaparkan ialah anggaran berasaskan koordinat menggunakan
konvensyen yang dipilih. Jadual rasmi zon, kawasan, masjid atau pihak
berkuasa agama perlu diutamakan apabila terdapat perbezaan yang penting.
Sesetengah pratetap dilabel sebagai anggaran keserasian kerana jadual awam
mungkin merangkumi peraturan institusi, musim, kawasan atau pembundaran yang
tidak dapat dihasilkan sepenuhnya oleh pengiraan koordinat ringkas.

My Prayer Times tidak bergabung dengan atau diperakui oleh mana-mana pihak
berkuasa atau organisasi yang dinamakan dalam aplikasi.

## Pemberitahuan dan Widget

Pemberitahuan waktu solat adalah pilihan. Jika diaktifkan, aplikasi mencipta
pemberitahuan setempat pada peranti untuk waktu yang anda pilih daripada
Subuh, Zohor, Asar, Maghrib dan Isyak. Penyampaian diuruskan oleh iOS, dan
membuka semula aplikasi secara berkala memperbaharui jadual berterusan.

Widget menggunakan petikan terkini yang disimpan oleh aplikasi utama: lokasi,
zon waktu, kaedah pengiraan pilihan, bahasa dan masa simpanan. Widget kecil
dan sederhana memfokuskan solat seterusnya dan waktu akan datang, manakala
widget besar memaparkan jadual lengkap bersama konteks tahrim. Aksesori Skrin
Kunci menyediakan paparan ringkas solat seterusnya.
